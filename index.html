THE TEN GODTEREST COMMANDMENTS

We have been long mislead on what it means to be in a relationship, why we should seek relationships, and how we should live them out. For too long, our team has pondered on the possibility of a guide book telling Christians how to build and maintain a healthy and meaningful relationship, and we're excited to let you know there is: The bible is that guide book!

You shall seek to be equally yolked:

: “You shall not date an unbeliever or a non-christian (2 Corinthians 6:14 Voice).”

Pray for guidance and wisdom before going on any date:

“You shall have a clear vision and purpose for dating (Proverbs 29:18 GW).”

Trust in God’s plan and purpose for your relationship:

“You shall both be led by the Spirit of God to date each other (Romans 8:14).”

Treat your date with respect, honor, and purity:

“You shall not love or date multiple partners but only one (Matthew 19:5-6 TPT).”

Seek authentic love:

: “You shall flee at the first sign of youthful lust (2 Timothy 2:22 NKJV).”

Practice self-control and avoid temptation:

: “You shall not lead yourselves totemptation by caressing and touching (Matthew 6:13 KJV).”

Communicate openly and honestly with your date:

: “You shall not lie to your partner concerning your health and medical conditions (Colossians 3:9 HCSB).”

Avoid pre-marital sex:

You shall both purpose in your heart not to defile yourself with premarital sex. (Daniel 1:8 KJV).

Seek Godly counsel and accountability:

“You shall have a Godly mentor for guidance and counseling (Proverbs 15:22 AMP.).”

Have a God centered relationship:

“You shall both seek the will of God concerning your future (1 Kings 22:5 TLB)”.
